The solution

How does it work?

Step 1 · Detect

Every short-date detected daily. Zero Expiry

Automated scans flag products approaching expiry - in full compliance with Selgros' freshness charter. Shelf location pinpointed so staff act in minutes.

Step 2 · Decide

AI sets the right discount, per SKU

Precise markdown per product, based on sales velocity, per store, per SKU, per day. No more empirical judgment.

Step 3 · Execute

Label printed, sale traced end-to-end

Price labels printed automatically. Every markdown traced from shelf to checkout.

Step 4 · Measure

Weekly KPIs, by store, from day one

Rescue rate, markdown profitability and expiry waste - tracked in real time. Winning practices replicated across the network.

Full compliance with the German Lebensmittelverschwendungsreduzierungsgesetz, every markdown action is logged and traceable.
